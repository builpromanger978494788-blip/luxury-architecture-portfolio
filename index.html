Our Story

Born from a passion for spaces that move the soul.

Architecture Studio was founded in 2006 with a singular conviction: that the spaces we inhabit shape who we become. From our studios in New York, Oslo, and Milan, we've spent nearly two decades translating this belief into homes, towers, and interiors that endure.

Our practice is built on deep collaboration — with clients, with craftspeople, with the contexts in which we work. We listen before we design, and we design before we draw.

What Drives Us

Principles of
Practice

Restraint as a design tool

We believe the most powerful designs are often those that know what to leave out. Restraint sharpens the focus on what truly matters — material, light, proportion, and the human experience within.

Materials as memory

Every material carries history. We source with intention and specify with care, selecting surfaces that will age gracefully and develop patina over time — becoming richer with the lives lived within them.

Light as architecture

Natural light is our most important collaborator. We orient spaces to capture its quality at different moments of day, letting shadow and brightness choreograph the emotional character of each room.
